How to Book Your Train Ticket

  1. Step 1: Sign up on our platform using your details.
  2. Step 2: Log in to your account.
  3. Step 3: Navigate to the "Book Ticket" section using the button in the navbar.
  4. Step 4: Fill in the required details, such as journey date, destination, and passenger information.
  5. Step 5: An admin will contact you via WhatsApp and email for confirmation.
  6. Step 6: Complete the payment to the admin as instructed.
  7. Step 7: The admin will send your ticket to your registered WhatsApp or email.

Note: Ensure your contact details are accurate to avoid any delays.
